ABOUT

This Movie Ticketing System is a web-based platform designed to allow users to purchase movie tickets in advance using virtual tickets. Instead of seat selection, the system focuses on ticket availability management to prevent tickets from going out of stock during peak times.

EFFICIENT FRIENDLY

Tickets are sold virtually USING QR CODES AND A HEXA CODE AS A UNIQUE IDENTIFICATION OF THAT TICKET and registered in advance to ensure availability.  By tracking ticket purchases in real time, the system helps prevent tickets from going out of stock during peak movie schedules.

TICKET UNIQUE SECURITY

The system provides a fast, reliable, and user-friendly way to secure movie tickets early.  It reduces missed screenings, improves demand management, and offers a smoother movie-going experience.
